Privacy

Spacewar collects nothing

Spacewar: Orbital Duel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device. It uses no analytics, no advertising, no accounts, and no network services. Nothing is gathered by the developer or shared with any third party.

Because the game gathers no data, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete. If Apple's TestFlight or the App Store collects anything as part of distributing the beta, that is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not this one.

Standing on shoulders

A modern tribute to Spacewar!, created in 1962 by Steve Russell and friends on MIT's PDP‑1, one of the first video games ever made. Sixty years on, the pull of the star still decides the duel.
